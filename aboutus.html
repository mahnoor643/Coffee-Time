ABOUT US






Our aim is to be the most in-depth, accurate, trusted, and most-visited source for coffee information. Coffee Times is a site for craft coffee enthusiasts around the world and is dedicated to serving the entire global coffee community through coffee education, promotion, and outreach. Coffee Times is a consumer-driven site, and we strive to remain unbiased in our ratings and editorial content. Put simply, we want it to be a community-maintained site, full of reviews and articles of the coffees people love (or don’t love) and to help people discover new amazing coffee roasters and learn more about coffee – one of the world’s most widely traded and consumed commodities!

Since May 2019, Coffee Guru has been an active forum for coffee lovers to come together and share opinions of coffees, and coffee retailers in a free environment. Established and maintained by dedicated volunteers, Coffee Guru is becoming the premier resource for consumer-driven coffee ratings, features on coffee culture and industry events, and coffee-related editorials. A vibrant community of hundreds of thousands of members from more than 100 countries have rated hundreds of different coffees around the world.

Coffee Times is dedicated to our core values:

Integrity – Fair and unbiased creation of the most reliable ratings and scores in the coffee industry.

Passion – An unmatched passion for the coffee industry and the best in it. Respect – Thoughtful regard for all our visitors, users and industry. Gratitude – A deep appreciation for our opportunity to serve, and for all those who’ve participated with us. Coffee Times is a community of coffee enthusiasts dedicated to the pursuit and appreciation of good coffee. Our mission is to continue to serve the coffee community as the premier resource for unbiased, consumer-driven information about coffee and roasters worldwide and to enhance the image and worldwide appreciation of coffee by: Providing a forum for coffee enthusiasts to share their opinions of the coffees they are drinking, discuss coffee and brewing, and discover new coffees and breweries; Encouraging our members to support and promote their favorite brewers and retailers of craft coffees; Conducting outreach to brewers, distributors, and retailers and extending their online opportunities.
At our company, we have a true passion for coffee and everything that goes into preparing the perfect cup. We believe that coffee is more than just a drink, it’s an experience. That’s why we have dedicated ourselves to sourcing the finest beans, roasting them to perfection, and brewing them in a way that highlights their unique flavors.
Our journey began years ago when we first fell in love with the taste and aroma of fresh coffee. We spent countless hours experimenting with different brewing methods, roasts, and grinds until we found what we believe is the perfect cup. We knew we had something special, and we wanted to share our passion with others.

Our coffee is hand-selected from the best farms around the world, ensuring that only the finest beans make it into your cup. We pride ourselves on our commitment to sustainability, so you can be confident that your coffee was grown and harvested in an eco-friendly way. Once we have the beans, we roast them in small batches to ensure that every bean is roasted to perfection. Our roastmasters meticulously monitor the roasting process to bring out the unique and complex flavors that make each bean special. We believe that the perfect roast is essential to unlocking the true potential of each bean, and we take that responsibility very seriously. We also believe that every brewing method has its strengths and weaknesses, so we offer a variety of brewing methods, including drip, pour-over, French press, and espresso. Our baristas are highly trained and experienced, ensuring that every cup is brewed to perfection. We use only the best water and adjust the temperature and brewing time according to the roast and brewing method, so you can be confident that your cup is of the highest quality. At our coffee shop, we are committed to creating a relaxing and welcoming environment where you can enjoy a delicious cup of coffee in the company of friends and colleagues. We believe that coffee has the power to bring people together, spark conversation, and inspire creativity. That’s why we choose to serve only the best, so you can enjoy a truly exceptional coffee experience every time you visit us.
At the end of the day, our passion for coffee is what has driven us to create the best coffee shop around. We believe that everything we do, from sourcing the beans to brewing the coffee, is an art, and we are proud to share our creations with the world. Come join us in exploring the world of coffee and discover why we truly believe that coffee is more than just a drink. .
